What is the Story

is a short literary genre in which events, whether real or entirely imaginary, are told within a specific time and place, with a small number of characters, and usually centered around a single event. The purpose of a short story is to convey events using a dense, simple, and compelling narrative to capture the reader's attention, provoke thought, or move them emotionally. Therefore, stories avoid unnecessary details; events are presented concisely and strikingly.

What is the Novel

A novel is a literary narrative genre, usually long and comprehensive, in which events, whether real or fictional, ranging from individual lives to social events are recounted in detail, enriched by elements of person, place, and time. In novels, the plot is much more complex and multilayered; the inner worlds of multiple characters, their thoughts, feelings, life struggles, and their relationships with the environment and society are explored in depth over extended periods of time, virtually inviting the reader into the world in which those characters live.
