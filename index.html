Kratos is a fictional character inspired by Greek mythology and the protagonist of the video game series "God of War." He is known for his rage, ruthless fighting skills, and tragic past. Raised as a ruthless warrior of Sparta, Kratos is betrayed by the gods who killed his family, leading him on a journey of vengeance and, in the process, into a relentless battle with the Olympian Gods. His character gains depth in later installments of the series; confronting his own mistakes and past, Kratos battles the gods of Norse mythology alongside his son, while also learning to control his anger.
